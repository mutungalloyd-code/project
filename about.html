Our Story

Rooted in Kenya.
Pressed with Purpose.

From a small Ruiru facility to shelves across Kenya — Ngao Beverages Ltd has spent over a decade turning the finest local fruits into drinks that are honest, healthy, and unapologetically Kenyan.

How It All Started

In 2015, James Mwangi — then a senior agricultural officer in Kiambu County — watched truckloads of ripe mangoes rot at farm gates because there was no reliable processing market. He quit his government post, pooled his savings, and rented a small facility in Ruiru.

His bet was simple: Kenyans deserved better than imported juice concentrates. The fruits were already here — on coastal mango farms, in the orange orchards of Murang'a, across the pineapple fields of the Coast. All they needed was a brand willing to press them properly.

Ngao Beverages — the name means "shield" in Swahili — was built to protect both the farmer's effort and the consumer's health. Today, we work with over 500 smallholder farmers and supply hotels, supermarkets, and retailers across Kenya.

Our Journey

A decade of pressing forward — one fruit, one farmer, one milestone at a time.

2015 — Founded

James Mwangi establishes Ngao Beverages in a rented facility in Ruiru, Kiambu. First product: Mango Bliss, cold-pressed from coastal Kenyan mangoes.

2016 — First Retail Partnership

Ngao products land on shelves at a Nairobi supermarket chain. Orders double within the first quarter of listing.

2018 — Dhahabu Orange Launched

Expansion into orange juice using fruits sourced from Murang'a and Nyeri. Amina Odhiambo joins as Head of Production.

2020 — Pwani Pineapple Introduced

Third product line launched during a challenging year. Pivots to direct hotel supply help sustain revenue through the pandemic period.

2022 — Eco Packaging Rollout

All bottles transition to 100% recyclable PET. Carbon emissions from the delivery fleet reduced by 30% through route optimization.

2024 — National Distribution

Brian Kipchoge joins as Sales & Distribution Manager. Ngao reaches retailers in Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and Eldoret.

2026 — Expanding the Farm Network

Active partnerships now cover 500+ smallholder farmers across 6 counties. New cold-storage facility planned for Mombasa.

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To transform Kenya's agricultural abundance into premium, minimally processed beverages — creating fair income for farmers, healthier choices for consumers, and a lower environmental footprint for everyone.

🌍 Our Vision

A Kenya where locally grown fruit powers a world-class beverage industry. We want Ngao to be the name that proves African produce, processed with care on African soil, can lead the market on quality.

🌱 Our Values

Transparency with farmers. Honesty on the label. Accountability in our supply chain. We don't add concentrates, artificial flavours, or preservatives — and we say so clearly.

🤝 Our Promise

Every bottle of Ngao juice pays a fair price back to the farm it came from. Our farmer payment rates are set above the market average — not as charity, but as good business.
