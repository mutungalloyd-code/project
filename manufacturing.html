The Six-Step Cold-Press Process

Every step is deliberate. Every decision is about keeping the fruit as close to its natural state as possible.

1
🌾
Sourcing

We source directly from over 500 smallholder farmers across Kilifi, Kwale, Murang'a, and Nyeri. Each farm is visited and vetted by our sourcing team. We pay above-market rates at the farm gate — no middlemen, no delays.

2
🚛
Transport & Intake

Harvested fruit is transported to our Ruiru facility in refrigerated vehicles within hours of picking. On arrival, every batch is weighed, logged, and inspected by our quality team before it enters the production floor.

3
🚿
Washing & Sorting

Fruit goes through a three-stage wash using food-grade water — a rinse, a sanitising bath, and a clean water final wash. Damaged or substandard fruit is sorted out by hand at this stage. Only the best moves forward.

4
⚙️
Cold-Pressing

Fruit is loaded into our hydraulic cold-press machines, which apply thousands of pounds of pressure to extract juice — with zero heat. This preserves the vitamins, enzymes, and natural flavour that heat pasteurisation destroys.

5
🔬
Quality Testing

Every batch is tested in our on-site lab before bottling. We check pH levels, Brix (sugar content), microbial counts, and colour consistency. Batches that don't meet our standards are rejected — no exceptions.

6
🍾
Bottling & Distribution

Approved juice is bottled in our clean-room facility into 250ml, 500ml, and 1L recyclable PET bottles. Bottles are sealed, labelled, and moved to refrigerated storage within the same day — ready for dispatch to retailers.

Quality Assurance

Quality at Ngao is not a department — it is a mindset that runs through every person on the production floor. Our Head of Production, Amina Odhiambo, built our QA framework from scratch using international food safety standards adapted for the Kenyan context.

🏅 Our Standards

All Ngao products are produced in compliance with Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS) guidelines for fruit juice. Our facility is inspected annually and holds a valid food manufacturing licence from the Kenya Food and Drug Authority.

🧪 Batch pH & Brix Testing

Every batch is tested for acidity and natural sugar levels before bottling. This ensures flavour consistency across every bottle of the same product.

🦠 Microbial Count Checks

We test for total plate count, yeast, and mould in every batch. Any batch exceeding our safety thresholds is discarded — it never reaches a shelf.

🎨 Colour & Clarity Grading

Juice colour and clarity are visually graded against our internal standards. Cold-pressed juice should look natural — not artificially bright or cloudy.

📦 Seal & Label Integrity

Every sealed bottle is checked for cap integrity and label accuracy before it leaves the facility. Mislabelled or improperly sealed bottles are pulled from the line.

❄️ Cold Chain Verification

Temperature logs are maintained from production through to delivery. Our distribution vehicles are equipped with real-time temperature monitoring.
