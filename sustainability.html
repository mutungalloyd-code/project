Our Commitment

Growing Green.
Staying Green.

Sustainability at Ngao is not a marketing angle — it is how we operate. From the farms we source from to the bottles we use, every decision is made with the environment and our communities in mind.

Three Pillars of Our Sustainability Strategy

Everything we do sits on three foundations. Together they define what it means to be a responsible Kenyan beverage company.

🌾

Local Sourcing

Every fruit in every Ngao bottle comes from a Kenyan farm. We partner directly with over 500 smallholder farmers across six counties — paying above-market rates at the farm gate and eliminating the middlemen who eat into farmer income. Buying local reduces food miles, keeps money in Kenyan communities, and means our fruit arrives fresher than anything imported could.

♻️

Eco Packaging

Since 2022, all Ngao bottles are made from 100% recyclable PET plastic. Our labels use water-based inks — no solvents, no heavy metals. We are currently piloting returnable glass bottles with select Nairobi retailers, with a full rollout planned for 2027. Our carton packaging uses FSC-certified paper from sustainably managed forests.

🌍

Low-Carbon Operations

Our Ruiru facility runs on a 40kW solar array that covers 60% of our daytime energy needs. Our delivery fleet has been optimised for route efficiency — reducing total kilometres driven by 22% since 2023. We are working toward a fully electric last-mile delivery fleet in Nairobi by 2028, starting with two electric vans in Q3 2026.

Voice From the Farm

Sustainability only means something if the people at the start of the supply chain feel it. Here is what one of our farmers has to say.

Before Ngao came, I was selling my mangoes to brokers who would pay whatever they felt like on the day. Some seasons I lost more than I earned because the price dropped after I had already harvested. Now I know the price before I pick a single fruit. Ngao sends someone to my farm, we agree a rate, and they pay within three days of collection. I have been able to buy a water pump and expand my orchard from two acres to five. My children are in school. That is what a fair price does.

Samuel Mwenda
Mango farmer, Kilifi County — Ngao supplier since 2017

What's in Our Packaging

Every material choice we make is deliberate. Here is what goes into the packaging around every Ngao bottle.

🧴
100% Recyclable PET

All bottles are made from food-grade PET plastic that is accepted by Nairobi's recycling infrastructure.

🖨️
Water-Based Inks

Labels are printed with water-based inks — no solvents, no heavy metals, and fully compatible with recycling.

📦
FSC-Certified Cartons

Outer cartons use paper from FSC-certified sources — meaning the forests they come from are responsibly managed.

🍶
Glass Pilot (2027)

We are trialling returnable glass bottles with select retailers. Full rollout planned once the return logistics are proven.
