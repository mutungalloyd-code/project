🥤 Products

Yes — completely. Every Ngao juice is made from whole Kenyan fruit with nothing added and nothing removed except the pulp. No concentrates, no artificial flavours, no preservatives, no added sugar, no colour enhancers. What you see on the label is exactly what is in the bottle.

Once opened, Ngao juices should be consumed within 2–3 days and kept refrigerated at all times. Because we use no preservatives, the juice is at its nutritional peak within the first 24 hours of opening. Unopened, our juices have a shelf life of 18–21 days when kept between 2–4°C.

🛒 Ordering

Orders can be placed by contacting us directly via phone (+254 700 000 000), email (info@ngaobeverages.co.ke), or through our contact form on this website. For retail orders, we recommend going through your nearest regional distributor for faster processing and delivery.

Minimum orders depend on your region and distributor. Generally, regional distributors require a minimum of 30–50 units per SKU per order. For direct orders from Ngao, the minimum is a full carton per product line. Individual consumers can find our products at retail stockists across Kenya.

We currently deliver to Nairobi (same day), Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and Eldoret (48 hours). If you are in a county not yet covered by our network, contact us — we are expanding our distribution footprint and may be able to arrange delivery or connect you with a nearby stockist.

🌱 Sustainability

Yes. Since 2022, all Ngao bottles are made from 100% recyclable PET plastic that is accepted by Nairobi's recycling infrastructure. Our labels use water-based inks with no heavy metals, making them safe for recycling. We are also piloting returnable glass bottles with select retailers, with a wider rollout planned for 2027.

Yes — we welcome visits from retailers, distributors, institutional partners, and students. Visits must be arranged in advance. Use our contact form and select "Book a Factory Visit" as your subject. Our team will confirm a suitable date and send you directions to our Ruiru facility.

We set our farm-gate prices at a minimum of 15% above the prevailing market rate and publish these rates to all farmers before the harvest season begins. Payment is made within three days of fruit collection — with no deductions for brokerage or transport, as we handle both ourselves. Two farmer representatives sit on our sustainability working group and have a direct line to our CEO.
