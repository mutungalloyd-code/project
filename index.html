Who We Are

Ngao Beverages Ltd is a Nairobi-based beverage manufacturer dedicated to producing high-quality, cold-pressed juices and drinks using locally sourced Kenyan fruits. We partner with smallholder farmers across Kenya to bring fresh, natural flavours to your table.
