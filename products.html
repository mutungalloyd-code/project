Why Cold-Pressed?

Most commercial juice is made using heat pasteurisation — which kills bacteria, yes, but also destroys much of the nutritional value. Cold-pressing is different.

🧬
Nutrients Preserved

No heat means vitamins, enzymes, and antioxidants stay intact — exactly as nature packed them.

🍓
Truer Flavour

Heat alters taste. Cold-pressing keeps the juice tasting like the fruit it came from — nothing added, nothing lost.

🚫
No Additives Needed

When you start with good fruit and press it right, you don't need preservatives, concentrates, or flavour enhancers.

⚡
Higher Yield per Fruit

Cold-press hydraulics extract up to 35% more juice per fruit than centrifugal methods — less waste, more value for farmers.
